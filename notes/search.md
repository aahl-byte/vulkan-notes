# search

full-text search across every page. results show where a match lives — its
<em>domain · phase · chapter</em> and the <em>heading › sub-heading</em> trail — so you
can tell a foundation mention from a synthesis one at a glance. click a result to land
on the exact section.

<div id="notes-search"></div>

tips:

- search is full-text: match on a term, a phrase, or a component name.
- nothing found? try the plain-language word for the idea, not the jargon — the notes
  introduce the analogy before the precise term.
- still stuck? skim **foundation** for the mental model, then narrow down.
