# search

use the box in the sidebar to search every page by full text. results show the
matching heading and a snippet — click through to land on the exact section.

tips:

- search is <em>full-text</em>: match on a term, a phrase, or a component name.
- nothing found? try the plain-language word for the idea, not the jargon — the notes
  introduce the analogy before the precise term.
- still stuck? skim **foundation** for the mental model, then narrow down.
